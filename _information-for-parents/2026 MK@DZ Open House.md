<h3><strong>Dazhong Primary School Open House 2026</strong></h3>
<p>Looking for the right start for your child's primary school journey?
<br>
<br>Join us at our Dazhong Open House to discover our vibrant learning environment,
meet our student leaders, and learn more about the exciting opportunities
we offer for every child during our school tour.
<br>
<br>Dates: 13th July &amp; 14th July (Monday and Tuesday)
<br>Venue: Dazhong Primary School
<br>Session 1:10.30 am to 11.30 am
<br>Session 2: 11.30 am to 12.30 pm
<br>
<br>Each session includes a guided school tour, interactive zones and a Q&amp;A
segment.
<br>
<br>Online Registration is required.
<br>
<br>Please register your attendance and select your preferred date and session
using the link below. Spaces are limited and allocated on a first-come,
first-served basis.
<br>
<br>Register here: <strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/dzpoh" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/dzpoh</a></strong>
<br>
<br>We look forward to welcoming you and your family to Dazhong Primary School
where Every Student is a High Potential Learner, an Ace Leader and a Greenovator!
<br>
</p>
