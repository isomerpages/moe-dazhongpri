### Swimsafer

The Swimsafer programme aims to teach swimming proficiency and water survival skills to children to minimize drowning incidences in a fun manner.Â  The programme takes place at the Primary 4 level. It is a compulsory programme for all students, as swimming is part of the PE syllabus rolled out by the Ministry. The programme is conducted by certified Swimsafer instructors engaged by the school.
