<h5><strong>GREENOVATORS @ DAZHONG</strong></h5>
<p><strong>(Aware, Active, and Advocating - Archers of Sustainability Aiming for a Greener Tomorrow)</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<h5>Why Greenovators @ Dazhong?</h5>
<p>Climate change is a significant threat to Singapore, making sustainable
development crucial for the next 50 years. Guided by the SG Green Plan
2030, our school’s <strong>Greenovators @ Dazhong Applied Learning Programme (ALP)</strong> empowers
students with STEM skills, knowledge, and develops their values and dispositions
for the 21<sup>st</sup> century.</p>
<p>Through the ALP, students learn sustainability using the 3As approach—<strong>Aware</strong>, <strong>Action</strong>,
and <strong>Advocacy</strong>—alongside the <strong>4Ds Design Thinking</strong> protocol: <strong>Discover</strong>, <strong>Define</strong>, <strong>Develop</strong>,
and <strong>Deliver, </strong>to develop a deep understanding of the environmental
challenges around them<strong>.</strong>. These core principles guides
students in problem-solving, fostering critical, adaptive and inventive
thinking. Students investigate sustainability challenges, develop solutions,
and test prototypes to create lasting change.</p>
