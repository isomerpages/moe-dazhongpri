<p><strong><u>Student Leadership @ Dazhong</u></strong>
<br>
</p>
<p>At Dazhong Primary School, Student Leadership is an integral part of our
holistic education approach. Guided by our belief in <strong>“Every Student, An Ace Leader”</strong>,
we are committed to nurturing confident, compassionate and responsible
leaders who lead with purpose, character and heart.</p>
<p>We believe that leadership is not defined by titles or positions, but
by everyday actions, values and the willingness to serve others. Through
purposeful opportunities and authentic experiences, every student is empowered
to discover their strengths, develop their potential and make a positive
difference in the school community.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Our Philosophy &amp; Vision</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Vision:</strong>
<br><em>Every Student, An Ace Leader</em>
</p>
<p>Our Student Leadership philosophy is anchored in the belief that <strong>every child has the capacity to lead</strong>.
Leadership is seen as a journey of growth, where students learn to lead
themselves, serve others and contribute meaningfully to their community.</p>
<p>By providing structured guidance, authentic platforms and progressive
leadership opportunities, we aim to nurture students who demonstrate confidence,
responsibility, empathy and resilience in their daily actions.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Our Values &amp; Framework</u></strong>
<br>
</p>
<p>Our Student Leadership programme is anchored on the <strong>A.R.C.H.E.R values</strong> — <strong>Ace, Respect, Curiosity, Humility, Empathy and Resilience</strong>.
These values shape our students’ character, guide their behaviours and
inform their decision-making as leaders and role models.</p>
<p><strong>Mission:</strong>
<br><em>Empowering Every Student to Lead with Purpose, Character, and Heart through the 3E approach, anchored on A.R.C.H.E.R values and the M.I.C.E.E Model.</em>
</p>
<p>Through the <strong>3E approach (Empower, Engage, Enable) </strong>and
the <strong>M.I.C.E.E Model</strong>, students are systematically guided
to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>develop self-leadership and responsibility,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>build strong interpersonal and communication skills, and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>grow into reflective leaders who serve with integrity and compassion.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Dazhong Leadership Framework</u></strong>
<br>
<br>To ensure that <strong>every student is provided with meaningful leadership opportunities</strong>,
the school adopts a tiered leadership pathway that supports progressive
development, inclusivity and holistic growth.
<br>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/PictureSL1.jpg">
</div>
<p><strong>Tier 1 – Leading Self (Mass Leadership: Class and CCA-based Leadership Roles)</strong>
<br>All students are provided opportunities to take on leadership responsibilities
within their classes. Through these roles, students develop self-management,
teamwork, responsibility and respect for others.</p>
<p><strong>Tier 2 – Leading Others (Student Leaders)</strong>
<br>Student leaders serve the wider school community through further leadership
roles, peer support, supporting and leading events. These authentic platforms
enable students to practise leadership skills, demonstrate service and
contribute actively to school life.</p>
<p><strong>Tier 3 – Leading Change (Student Leader Council)</strong>
<br>Members of the Student Leaders’ Council take on higher-level responsibilities
in leadership planning, coordination, representation and student voice.
Working closely with teachers, they lead initiatives and contribute to
school development, fostering a strong sense of ownership and responsibility.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Dazhong Leadership in Action</u></strong>
<br>
<br>Leadership development begins with a <strong>formal induction through the Student Leaders’ Investiture</strong>,
where students are entrusted with their roles and take a pledge to uphold
the school’s values and serve with integrity. This marks the start of their
leadership journey and reinforces a strong sense of identity, responsibility,
and commitment.
<br>
</p>

<p>Throughout the year, student leaders are provided with authentic platforms
to practise leadership in meaningful contexts. These include:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Hosting foreign school visits</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Performing weekly leadership duties during morning assemblies, including
commanding, flag-raising and peer support as well as supporting peers through
recess duties</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Serving during key school events such as Teachers’ Day celebrations, Open
House, Sports Day, festive occasions, NE/SS events and more</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participating in Student Leadership workshops and development programmes
to strengthen leadership competencies, values-based decision-making and
character development</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through these experiences, students develop confidence, communication
skills, teamwork and empathy, while learning to serve others with respect
and responsibility.</p>

<p><strong><u>Our Aspiration</u></strong>
<br>
<br>Through a strong culture of Student Leadership, we aspire to nurture <strong>responsible citizens and compassionate leaders</strong> who
are prepared to contribute meaningfully to their school and community.</p>
<p>At Dazhong Primary School, we believe that when students are given trust,
guidance and opportunities, <strong>every child can rise to lead with confidence, character and heart.</strong>
<br>
