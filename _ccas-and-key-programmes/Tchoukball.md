<p><strong>Tchoukball</strong>
<br>
<br>The Tchoukball CCA provides students with opportunities to learn and participate
in a team sport in a structured and supportive environment.</p>
<p>The CCA emphasises sports values such as teamwork, respect, perseverance,
and fair play, guiding students to develop positive character and strong
interpersonal skills.</p>
<p>The aim of the Tchoukball CCA is to develop students’ interest and competence
in the sport, with opportunities to participate in the National School
Games (NSG) for exposure and recognition.</p>
<p>The CCA adopts an approach that focuses on character building alongside
performance development, supporting students’ holistic growth through sport.</p>
