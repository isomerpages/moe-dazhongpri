<p><strong><u>Kindergarten Care (KCare)</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Kindergarten Care (KCare), provided by Commit Learning Schoolhouse (CLS)
is available for parents who require a <strong>full-day service</strong> for
your child who is enrolled in MOE Kindergarten (MK).</p>
